How to Play Boat Ride 2.0
Supreme Boat Captain Race

🏆 Goal: Become the Supreme Boat Captain!

Complete all 4 challenges — Boat School, buy a Boat, catch Fish, and perform Rescues — then race to the Winner's Dock and land on ⭐ WIN with a positive balance.

📋 THE 4 CHALLENGES
🎓

1. Boat School

Earn 3 stars (or 2 with Shortcut). Must graduate before buying a boat.

🛥️

2. The Boat Store

Roll through and buy a boat. Pay $100 to bypass if not graduated.

🐟

3. Catch a Fish

Collect 10 fish (or 6 with Shortcut). Accumulates across visits.

🆘

4. Let's Rescue!

Perform 2 rescues (or 1 with Shortcut). One space gives a $20 tip!

🎴 Shipwreck Cards
Buy 1 card/turn for $10. Play 1 card/turn. Cards bought this turn can't be played until next turn.

💰 Highway Robbery — steal all money | ⚡ Shortcut — reduce a challenge | 🌀 Teleportation — jump anywhere | ❄️ Freeze Frame — skip 2 turns | 🎲 Double Time — 2 bonus rolls | 🚀 Fast Track — no exact roll to WIN
💸 Debt Rules
Balance can go negative but you can't win in debt. 5% interest each turn in debt. Debt Relief: Sacrifice a challenge to reset to $0.
🤝 Trading
Propose trades before rolling: money, fish, rescues, stars, or cards. Accept, Decline, or Counter-offer.
📧 Online Multiplayer & Notifications
Email notifications fire when it's your turn and you're away from the tab. Works in incognito too — just sign in each session. Notifications go to your account email.

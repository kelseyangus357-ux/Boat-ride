How to Play

🏆 Goal: Become the Supreme Boat Captain!

Complete all 4 challenges, then race to the Winner's Dock and land on ⭐ WIN with a positive balance!

🎓

Boat School

Earn 3 ⭐ stars (or 2 with a shortcut card). You must complete this before buying a boat. Visit every lap until graduated.

🛥️

The Boat Store

Buy a boat 🚣🛶⛵🚢! Must be graduated first, or pay 💰$100 to bypass. Each boat has a unique price and icon.

🐟

Catch a Fish

Collect 10 🐟 fish (or 6 with a shortcut). Land on fishing spaces and work your way through the jut to catch fish.

🆘

Let's Rescue!

Perform 2 🆘 rescues (or 1 with a shortcut). Visit the rescue jut and save swimmers in need!

🃏 Shipwreck Cards
Buy 1 card per turn for 💰$10. Play 1 card per turn. Cards bought this turn can't be played until next turn.

💰 Highway Robbery — steal ALL money from a player
⚡ Shortcut — reduce a challenge requirement
🌀 Teleportation — jump to any space
❄️ Freeze Frame — freeze a player for 2 turns
🎲 Double Time — get 2 bonus rolls
🚀 Fast Track — no exact roll needed to WIN
💸 Debt Rules
Your balance can go negative. You cannot win or enter the Winner's Dock with a negative balance.
5% interest is charged each turn while in debt.
Debt Relief: Sacrifice a completed challenge to reset balance to 💰$0.
🤝 Trading
Before rolling, you can propose a trade with another player. Offer money 💰, fish 🐟, rescues 🆘, or shipwreck cards 🃏. The other player can accept, decline, or make a counter-offer.
🏆 Winner's Dock
Once all 4 challenges are complete, you enter the Winner's Dock. Roll the exact number to reach the ⭐ WIN space (unless you have Fast Track). Watch out — you can lose turns or go backwards!
